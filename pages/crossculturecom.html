Cross Cultute Communication

Learning Objective

To understand how culture influences communication and reasoning styles, motivation, professional and social relationships, business protocol and practices and personal lifestyles

Achievement

  • To be able to understand and assess yourself from a global cultural perspective
  • To acquire skills to solve problems through discussions with people with different backgrounds
  • To be able to create a cultural gap analysis between your and another culture
  • To value and leverage cultural differences

Learner Profile

All leaders with limited to some overseas experience, limited to no working or engagement experience together with persons from different cultures
