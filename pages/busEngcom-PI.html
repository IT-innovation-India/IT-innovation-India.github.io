Business English Communication (Pre-Intermediate)

Learning Objective

To develop the basic business communication skills to participate effectively in a global business environment

Achievement

  • To gain confidence to begin expressing opinions and thoughts in English
  • To confidently present yourself and your organization
  • To overcome hesitation and enjoy speaking in English

Learner Profile

All leaders with limited overseas experience limited capability in English communication and a TOEIC score of below 600
