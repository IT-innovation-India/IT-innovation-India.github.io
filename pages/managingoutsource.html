Managing Outsource/Offshore Project

Learning Objective

Through practical experience, learn ways of overcoming the many challenges and failures in global project outsourcing management including client and internal negotiations, sudden scope changes, risk management, cross culture issues and so on.

Achievement

  • To be able to gather client requirements, make estimations, project planning & schedule tracking, risk management, global communication & collaboration
  • To overcome project challenges and obstacles during project execution, experience such as requirements change, absence of main stakeholders, incorrect planning & estimations and so on
  • To manage the relationship with both client and offshore development team

Learner Profile

Working experience – 3-8 years ｜ Exposure to Complete Software Development Life Cycle activities. Familiarity with multi-person projects & Team handling role
Communication capability – MED
