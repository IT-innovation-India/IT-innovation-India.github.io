Corporate Internship

Learning Objective

To get an in-depth knowledge, skills & techniques of the formal functional activities of a participating global organization and to understand informal organizational interrelationships from a cross-culture perspective.

Achievement

  • To create empathy with other functional leaders in global environment
  • To be able to communicate effectively in-regards to a functional activity
  • To be able to influence others in a cross-culture environment
  • To achieve business output at the participating organization
